The mechanism

No reporter. No referee. Just the record.

When a market closes, the contract fetches the page you named and a diverse validator set reads it independently. They must converge on the same answer before escrow moves — the Equivalence Principle, applied to truth itself.

  • Reads any public URL on-chain
  • Validators agree, or the market voids
  • Winners split the losing pool, pro-rata
